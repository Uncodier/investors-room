## Revenue Model Overview

Market Fit operates a **multi-tiered SaaS model** designed for scalability and growth, with pricing that aligns with customer value creation and market positioning.

## Pricing Tiers

### Commissionist Plan - $0/month
Pay just for results and scale on demand

- 10% Sales Fee on generated revenue
- 3% Retention Fee on recurring customers
- Community Access and resources
- Standard Support via chat/email
- 3-year partnership contract
- 100 Campaign Credits included
- Performance-based scaling

### Startup Plan - $99/month
Only available 7 days after account creation

- 5% Sales Fee on generated revenue
- 1.5% Retention Fee on recurring customers
- Advanced AI predictions and targeting
- Priority support and onboarding
- Team collaboration tools
- 1,000 Campaign Credits included
- Campaign investment co-funding available

### Enterprise Plan - $499/month
Only available 60 days after startup plan

- 1% Sales Fee on generated revenue
- Topped Annual Fees (no retention fees)
- Full API access and custom integrations
- Advanced Integrations with CRM/ERP systems
- 24/7 priority support and dedicated CSM
- Training Sessions and certification programs
- Unlimited Campaign Credits
- Co-investment in high-performing campaigns

## Revenue Share Model

Our innovative revenue share approach aligns our success with our clients' growth:

### Performance-Based Pricing
- **Sales Fees**: 1-10% of revenue generated through our platform
- **Retention Fees**: 1.5-3% of recurring customer value
- **Success Alignment**: We only profit when our clients succeed

### Campaign Investment Program
- **Co-funding**: Market Fit invests in high-potential campaigns
- **Risk Sharing**: Shared investment reduces client acquisition costs
- **ROI Focus**: Campaigns optimized for measurable returns
- **Scale Benefits**: Larger investments for proven strategies
