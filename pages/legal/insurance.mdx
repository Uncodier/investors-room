## Risk Management Framework

### Risk Assessment Process
- **Quarterly Reviews**: Planned comprehensive risk assessments
- **Risk Register**: Will be implemented post-fundraising
- **Mitigation Plans**: Action plans for identified risks
- **Board Oversight**: Regular board risk committee meetings

### Key Risk Categories
| Risk Category | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---------------|------------|--------|------------|
| Cyber Security | Medium | High | Insurance + Security controls |
| Data Breach | Low | High | Privacy program + Cyber insurance |
| Key Person Loss | Medium | Medium | D&O insurance + Succession planning |
| Product Liability | Low | Medium | Professional liability + QA processes |
