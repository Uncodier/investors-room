## Major Press Releases

### 2024 Announcements

#### Series B Funding Announcement
**Date**: March 15, 2024  
**Headline**: "Market Fit Raises $25M Series B to Accelerate AI-Powered Experimentation Platform"

Market Fit, the leading AI-powered A/B testing and experimentation platform, today announced the completion of its $25 million Series B funding round. The round was led by Sequoia Capital with participation from existing investors First Round Capital and Andreessen Horowitz.

#### Product Launch: AI Experiment Designer
**Date**: February 8, 2024  
**Headline**: "Market Fit Launches Revolutionary AI Experiment Designer, Reducing Test Setup Time by 80%"

Market Fit unveiled its groundbreaking AI Experiment Designer, which uses machine learning to automatically generate, configure, and optimize A/B tests based on business objectives and historical data.

#### Enterprise Milestone
**Date**: January 22, 2024  
**Headline**: "Market Fit Surpasses 120 Enterprise Customers, Reaches $12M ARR Milestone"

The company announced reaching significant growth milestones, including 120+ enterprise customers and $12 million in annual recurring revenue, representing 300% year-over-year growth.

Market Fit completed its Series A funding round, enabling the company to expand its engineering team and accelerate product development.

#### Partnership Announcements
