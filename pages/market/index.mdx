## Market Drivers

### 🚀 AI Revolution in Sales
- 94% of sales organizations plan to adopt AI by 2025
- Generative AI making human-like conversations possible
- Demand for always-on customer engagement

### 💰 Sales Efficiency Crisis
- Average sales rep only spends 33% of time selling
- Cost per sales hire exceeding $100K in tech
- 50%+ annual turnover in sales roles

### 📈 Infinite Growth Demands
- Pressure to scale without proportional headcount growth
- Need for 24/7 global coverage
- Expectation of instant response times

### 🤝 Changing Buyer Behavior
- 70% of B2B buyers prefer digital interactions
- Expectation of personalized, immediate responses
- Self-service buyer journey requiring nurture at scale
